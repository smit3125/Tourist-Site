Commercial Capital of Gujarat

Ahmedabad is the largest city in the State of Gujarat.The City established itself as the home of a booming textile industry, which earned the nickname “The Manchester of India”. With the creation of the state of Gujarat in 1960, Ahmedabad gained prominence as the political and commercial capital of the state.
